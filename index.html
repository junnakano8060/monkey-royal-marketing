Common Questions

What does Monkey Royal Marketing do?

We help local and small businesses improve their online visibility so potential customers can find them more easily on Google, Google Maps, and AI-powered search results. Our core services include Google Business Profile optimization, AEO, SEO, and social media support.

Who is this service for?

Local businesses, small businesses, and service-area business owners who want more visibility, calls, bookings, and customers — without the confusion of managing digital marketing alone.

How much does the basic GBP Optimization cost?

Our basic GBP Optimization package starts at $300. For advanced services like AEO, SEO, social media, or ongoing visibility management, we review your situation first and provide a custom recommendation.

Do you guarantee results?

We do not guarantee specific rankings, lead counts, or revenue. What we guarantee is a clear process, defined deliverables, transparent reporting, and practical work focused on improving your online visibility.

What is AEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization. It helps structure your business information so AI-powered search tools can better understand what you do, who you serve, and why you are relevant — making it easier for customers using AI search to find you.
